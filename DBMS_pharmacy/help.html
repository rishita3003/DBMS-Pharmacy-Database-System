Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I make an appointment?

    • You can schedule an appointment by calling our appointment hotline at 1850-470-2407.

  • What should I bring to my first visit?

    • Please bring your identification, insurance information, any relevant medical records, and a list of medications you are currently taking.

  • Can I access my medical records online?

    • Yes, patients can access their medical records through our secure patient portal. If you need help setting up your account please contact our support team.

    Patient Support Services

    Our Patient Support Services team is here to assist you with any non-medical questions, guide you through our hospital's amenities, and provide information on local accommodations for families and visitors.
